Executive Summary

FSG asked us to start small, prove value fast, and build internal trust. This single offer focuses on practical training, two synthesis pilots, and IT thought partnership with only the governance needed to run safely.

Confident usage Better prompts and safer patterns across teams
Quick wins Interview and long-meeting synthesis that produce client-ready outputs
Enablement Office hours for IT, Copilot checklist, vendor-question playbook
Measured value Before/after results for both pilots and updated SOPs

Scope & Deliverables

Discovery interviews

  • 6–8 stakeholders to capture real workflows and edge cases
  • Pilot specs and acceptance tests agreed in Week 1

Training

  • Two live sessions, U.S. and India, 60–90 minutes each
  • Role-based quickstarts and prompt frameworks with recordings

Pilot A — Interview Synthesis Engine

  • Inputs: interview notes, transcripts, or audio
  • Outputs: themes map, quotes bank, insights brief, client-ready slides
  • Kit: prompt pack, SOP update, QA checklist

Pilot B — Long-Meeting Synthesis Agent

  • Inputs: long facilitated session transcripts and artifacts
  • Outputs: decision log, action tracker, draft comms, executive summary
  • Kit: prompt pack, SOP update, QA checklist

IT thought partnership

  • Office hours and rapid response for security posture and enablement
  • Copilot adoption checklist and vendor-question playbook

Light governance

  • One-page Rules of the Road and staff FAQ
  • Data-handling guardrails tailored to the two pilots
